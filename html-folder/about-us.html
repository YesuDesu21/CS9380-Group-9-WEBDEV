ABOUT
US

We are a group of students from Saint Louis University, currently pursuing a degree in BS in Computer Science. This website was developed as part of our academic project in Web Systems Development.

Our objective is to provide informative content about gender equality. By presenting researched insights, statistics, and case studies, we aim to promote awareness and encourage meaningful conversations on these issues.

While we are still students, we believe that creating platforms like this is an important step toward shaping a more equitable and inclusive future.

MEET THE TEAM

Feil Jasper Doria

"For generations, society has assigned certain roles and behaviors to men and women, expecting each to follow paths defined by gender. But the truth is, skills, interests, and ambitions are not bound by gender.

Progress happens when we break these stigmas and create spaces where everyone can grow, explore, and excel in any field they choose. Let’s continue to support a world where opportunities are defined by ability and passion, not stereotypes."

Abrelle Yin Lopez

"Gender equality and feminism are often misunderstood as misandry. In truth, they focus on addressing injustices and building a society where both men and women are empowered, supported, and uplifted."

Lawrence Brix Rillera

"Gender equality means giving everyone equal opportunities regardless of gender. By breaking stereotypes, promoting fairness, and supporting inclusive policies, we can build a more just and respectful society. It also empowers individuals to reach their full potential without limitations. Creating this balance benefits not only individuals but also strengthens communities as a whole. Achieving it requires collective effort and continuous awareness."

Adrian Jay Soralde

"One of the most important ideals that society should support is gender equality. Regardless of gender, I think everyone should have the opportunity to achieve their goals and be treated with dignity."

Hannah Parayno

"Gender equality means creating a society where everyone, regardless of gender, has the freedom to be strong, to be sensitive, and to pursue their ambitions. It’s not about favoring one gender over another, it’s about recognizing the full humanity and potential in all people, and ensuring everyone has the opportunity to thrive."
